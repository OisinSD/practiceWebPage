ABOUT Nook

Helping the community

Nook started as a small initiative to make books more accessible to communities with limited resources. The organization collects donated books from individuals, charities, businesses, and other organizations, aiming to give these books a new life while supporting education and reading. Over time, Nook grew and wanted to reach even more people, which led to the idea of creating an online platform. By selling books at extremely discounted prices through the website, Nook can now connect with a wider audience and continue its mission of spreading knowledge and helping those who might not otherwise have access to books.

Our Store


I created the Nook website to help TT (Tom Taylor), a not-for-profit organization, sell donated books online to support less fortunate communities. The website allows users to browse and purchase books easily, view book details, and stay updated with news. It also lets staff and volunteers update book listings and news, making it easy to manage. My goal in building the website was to make it simple, user-friendly, and accessible on both desktop and mobile devices, helping TT reach more people and achieve their mission of sharing books with those in need.
